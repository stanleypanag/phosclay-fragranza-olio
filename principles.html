Mission | Vision | Core Values

MISSION


Phosclay delivers high quality cosmetic products in satisfying clients, customers' needs and give essential products that can be used by our fellow customers, clients, employees, and ensure they have a healthy and productive life. Phosclay is a catalyst for change, socially responsible and advocate for a healthy environment.

VISION


Institute outstanding scientific excellence in cosmetic manufacturing competencies and enhance international scale up for return on investment for innovator companies.

CORE VALUES


GOOD HEART

We derived good heart as standard set by God to be a better company. We want to believe that people, in their hearts, are essentially good. Thus, it must include leadership within themselves and company.

COMMITMENT

We focus on a few key priorities and ensure we deliver with quality, on time, believe in the power of technology to enable progress. As we hold ourselves accountable to the bold commitments we make inside and outside of the office.

INTEGRITY

Our teams act with integrity and honesty, and focus on putting ourselves in the shoes of others. We’re growing this company not for own personal gain, but for each other and relentlessly focusing on our customer’s success, building high quality systems, and planning for long-term inside.
