HISTORY


A grandest masterpiece comes from a humble scratch. In 2012, our Perfumery business has started in Burol III, Dasmariñas City, Cavite with 40 different variants of perfumes, 4 Distributors and few resellers. On September 26, 2020, it is formally registered in DTI with BNN 2204797 as Phosclay Chemical Manufacturing (PCM), a newly formed Cosmetic company which is owned by Rakim N. Daurong, JD. and Ryan Tsubasa Rivas. It is a mission-driven company intended to create job opportunities for Filipino people. The company has strong core values- Good heart, Integrity, Commitment which comes from the Holy Bible.

Being one of the players in sales, marketing and distribution of Rubbing Ethyl and Isopropyl Alcohol, Dishwashing Liquid, Disinfectant, Alcologne, Alcogel, Liquid Handsoap, Fabric Conditioner, Hand and Hair Sanitizer, Inspired & Branded Perfumes, and Car Diffusers with rendering export services to clientele nationwide and international market. With over 70 staff, PCM has successfully established wholesale and retail products nationwide to accommodate small and medium sized companies. Our success is primarily based on client requirements to create true commercial value for the clients. Phosclay is a cosmetic manufacturing business based in Dasmarinas, Cavite. The Company helps individuals and also an environmentally benign and will provide opportunities to others.

The Intellectual Property Office of the Philippines registered the company on August 3, 2021, for a period of ten years as Branded Perfume. The Food and Drug Authority (FDA) also granted it a license to operate as Cosmetic Trader until October 20, 2023. The Business Permit and Licensing Office of Dasmarinas, Cavite, has granted it a permit to operate until December 31, 2022. At present, the company has blessed with nine (9) Investors, Nine (9) Provincial Distributors for perfumes such as Rizal, Nueva Ecija, Quezon Province, Batangas, Bulacan, Zambales, Davao and Pampanga, one (1) Stockist, more than 150 resellers including freelancers, online, and on-site marketers, and five (5) City Distributors.
